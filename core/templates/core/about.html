About Thelma

The hands behind every set.

From Busy to Bougee...

Thelma is a 22-year-old entrepreneur and University of Buea graduate who turned her passion for nail art into a thriving brand.

With a background in accounting, she brings the same precision and attention to detail from the classroom to every nail set she creates. What started as a hobby has grown into Pressed by Thelma — a brand trusted by hundreds of clients across Cameroon.

Based in Buea, Thelma believes that every woman deserves to feel beautiful, confident, and put-together — no matter how busy life gets. That's why she created press-on nails that are easy to apply, long-lasting, and absolutely stunning.
